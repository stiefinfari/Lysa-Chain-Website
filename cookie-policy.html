This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are and how we use them on the website lysachain.com.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are saved on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

How do we use cookies?

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Technical Cookies: Necessary for the operation of the site (e.g., navigation, access to secure areas). They do not require your consent.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the site by collecting information anonymously (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Third-Party Cookies: Our site includes third-party features (e.g., YouTube videos, Spotify/SoundCloud players) that may set cookies.

Managing Cookies

You can change your cookie preferences at any time through the banner on the site or via your browser settings. However, disabling some cookies may affect your browsing experience.
